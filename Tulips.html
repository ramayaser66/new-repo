Tulips

When it comes to these classic yellow blooms, there's way more than meets the eye!

Tulips are amongst the most popular of bulbs, valued for their brilliant flower colours and shapes. Plant in autumn for a show of spring flowers. Choose from a large range to suit the situation.

Quick facts

  1. Common name Tulip
  2. Botanical name Tulipa
  3. Group Bulbous perennial
  4. Flowering time Usually March to May
  5. Planting time October-November
  6. Height Varies, from 15cm (6in) to 75cm (30in); spread: 15cm (6in)
  7. Aspect Full sun
